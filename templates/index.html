More information

Watercooler is a great tool for chatting with your friends or collaborating with your coworkers. It's available anywhere you have an Internet connection and requires no software installation.

Features

  • Easy setup. Just name your conversation, give it a password, and share it with your friends.
  • Saved transcripts. Transcripts of your conversations are always available.
  • Persistent conversations. Resume a conversation at any time, even after weeks or months.
  • Searchable. Easily search your conversations.
  • Public conversations. Create public conversations that anyone can join.
  • Multimedia support. Links to images, videos, or audio files are viewable or playable right from the conversation itself.
